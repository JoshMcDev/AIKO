5616.601 Time and Material Contracts.

(Revised June 2015)

(a) In addition to the guidance for Time and Material (T&M) contracts found at FAR 16.601(d) and DFARS 216.601(d) , a formal Determination and Findings (D&F) is required when a contract contains a T&M CLIN.
