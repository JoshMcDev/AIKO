5603.104-4 Disclosure, Protection, and Marking of Contractor Bid or Proposal Information and Source Selection Information.

(Revised July 2014)

Contracting officers shall require individuals participating personally and substantially in a Federal agency procurement within the meaning of FAR subsection 3.104-3 , to sign the Non-Disclosure Agreement. SOAE, DDAE, MDAE, DOP, DDOP, PEO, HCD/FCOs, LNOs, Attorney Advisors, SSAC Pool Members, etc. may sign one annually (see Annual Non-Disclosure Agreement) and provide to KOs upon request for the contract file, rather than signing one on a procurement-by-procurement basis.

Parent topic: 5603.104 Procurement Integrity.
